Welcome to Hand Picked Auto!

Council Bluff, Iowa's trusted dealership for used cars, trucks, vans and SUVs.

At Hand Picked Auto, we work hard to provide the best selection of pre-owned vehicles at prices our customers can afford. We carry both domestic and imported used cars, from the classic and hard-to-find to the most populer models around. Our inventoy changes several times a week as we bring you bigger and better deals on used cars and trucks.

Online Inventory - Always Up-to-Date

To service our customers better, we've invested in the technology to keep our online inventory listing current. We showcase all of our used cars, trucks, vans, and SUVs on our website and update our online inventory daily. You can search our inventory – online – by make, model, year and price. We’ll provide you everything you need to know about our used cars. On our website, you can see everything from the used cars’ pictures, to the number of miles, transmission type, exterior and interior colors, features, price and any warranties and certifications.

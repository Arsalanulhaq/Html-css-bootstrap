About Us

At Classic Auto Sales, we provide the best customer service and largest selection of pre-owned vehicles in the Holliston, MA area. Customer satisfaction is something we take very seriously. We ask our customers to rate us, the service we provide, and how their cars perform. By working with our customers, we can improve our services and tailor our inventory to what our customers currently need. A car is an expensive purchase, which is why we do not take the process, or our customers, lightly. We value their input and ask that they help us help them. Let us know what you need, and we’ll do whatever we can to help. We invite you to get to know the Classic Auto Sales family.

Using Technology to Help You Find the Best Deals
We recognize that you lead a busy life and purchasing a car, even a pre-owned car, is a huge investment. You should have choices and access to a large selection of inventory. For this very reason, we’ve added an online-inventory system to our website, allowing you 24-hour access to view our pre-owned vehicles. You can search through our inventory in the matter of seconds. There’s no need to waste your time visiting different car lots, over and over again, looking for the right car when you can search from your home or office several times a week – we add pre-owned vehicles to our website daily. You can get a jump on owning the exact make and model you’ve been looking for – just by checking our inventory online.

Thank You
Each year we strive to improve our service and inventory, and we’ve found there is no better way to do this than by getting to know every one of our customers. To all of our customers, past and present, “Thank you!” for welcoming Classic Auto Sales into the Holliston community and making us an important part of your lives.

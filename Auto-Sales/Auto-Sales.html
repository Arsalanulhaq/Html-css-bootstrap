Welcome to Classic Auto Sales!

The premier dealership for quality pre-owned cars, trucks, vans, and SUVs for over twenty years.

At Classic Auto Sales, we work hard providing the largest selection of pre-owned vehicles to the public through our retail sales in Natick and now Holliston, Massachusetts. We carry both domestic and imported used cars, from the classic and hard-to-find to the most popular models around. Our inventory changes several times a week, as we bring you bigger and better deals on used cars and trucks, to provide the customer with the most affordable prices.

There are a number of highly qualified repair stations in our local area that can perform a comprehensive inspection, if desired. If you need to fly to Massachusetts to view or pick up your vehicle, we will be glad to pick you up from the Logan International Airport shuttle. Please call our office for details, as the shuttle is a fast and efficient way to get here.

Online Inventory - Always Up-to-Date
To service our customers better, we've invested in the technology to keep our online inventory listing current. We list our entire inventory online and update our online inventory daily. You can search our inventory by make, model, year, and price. Our company will provide you with everything you need to know about our used cars, including a free Carfax report. Through our website you can see everything from used vehicle pictures, to the number of miles, transmission, exterior and interior colors, features, price and any warranties and certifications.

Let Us Find the Perfect Used Car for You
If you can't find the used car you want in our inventory, give us a call. We have access to an extensive network of used cars throughout the state of Massachusetts and beyond. We will work hard to find just the used car you’re looking for.

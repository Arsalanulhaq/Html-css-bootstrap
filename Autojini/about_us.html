About Us

AutoJini focuses on drawing customers to your lot, driving sales and helping you manage your inventory and expenses. Our Virtual Showroom allows your customers to "visit" your dealership anytime of the day or night, from any location. This means expanding your market coverage, reaching customers across the country! When you consider that 80% of buyers search the internet for great deals, having an online presence is crucial to finding new customers and driving sales.

Approved by IIADA and IL-IADA

AutoJini.com is an approved vendor of Iowa Independent Automobile Dealers Association (IIADA) and Illinois Independent Automobile Dealers Association (IL-IADA). Our affiliation means that you are working with a firm which has established its reputation in the industry for providing professional web solutions, website design, web development, and website management to auto dealers. We are proud to support and provide quality dealer websites to the members of these associations and beyond.

Industry Leaders

We work closely with other industry leaders like AutoTrader.com and Cars.com, whose main goal is to connect buyers with sellers. We ensure that you get the best dealership website solution, outstanding service, and the personal attention your dealership deserves.
